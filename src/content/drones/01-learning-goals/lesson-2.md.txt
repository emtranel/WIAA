This curriculum aligns with the following NGSS standards:

1. **HS-PS3-1:** Energy in a system
2. **HS-PS4-2:** Electromagnetic radiation
3. **HS-ETS1-1:** Engineering design process
4. **HS-LS2-3:** Ecosystem dynamics
5. **HS-L4-1:** Systems and system models

These standards provide a framework for understanding the scientific principles underlying drone technology and its applications in agriculture.
