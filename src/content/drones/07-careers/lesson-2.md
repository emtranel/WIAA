## Build your skills starting now

### Fundamental Skills

- **STEM Classes:** Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Math - great and accessible ways to learn about the world around you and build up knowledge for future classes
- **Agriculture Experience:** Take agriculture classes, go on farm tours, or get a job at an agriculture company over the summer
- **Part 107 License:** Once you turn 16, you are eligible to get your Part 107 pilot's license
- **Data Analysis:** Learn skills with Excel, Google Sheets, or programming languages
- **Soft Skills:** Take leadership roles in class projects or clubs, build problem-solving skills by considering challenging clubs or activities, or experiment on your own to learn
